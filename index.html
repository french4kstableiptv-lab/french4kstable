Frequently Asked Questions

IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) delivers TV content through your internet connection rather than traditional satellite or cable. Simply install a compatible app on your device, enter the login credentials we provide, and you'll have instant access to thousands of channels and on-demand content from around the world.

Our service works with all popular IPTV apps including IPTV Smarters Pro, Tivimate, Televizo, XCIPTV, iMPlayer, and many more. For MAG devices, we provide a MAC address-based setup. We'll guide you through the best app choice during setup.

For smooth 4K streaming, we recommend a minimum of 25 Mbps. For Full HD content, 10-15 Mbps is sufficient. Our adaptive streaming technology automatically adjusts quality based on your connection, so even on slower connections you'll get a great experience.

Activation is typically within 1-5 minutes during business hours. We deliver your credentials via email and WhatsApp. In rare cases during high-demand periods, it may take up to 30 minutes, but we always strive for instant delivery.

Each standard subscription includes one simultaneous connection. However, you can install the service on as many devices as you want — you just can't watch on more than one at the same time. Need multiple simultaneous connections? Contact us for a custom plan.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfer, PayPal, and cryptocurrency. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to discuss the most convenient payment option for you.
